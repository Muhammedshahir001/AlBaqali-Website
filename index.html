About

Al Baqali Automatic Doors was established in Doha, Qatar in 1992 by Mr. Yaqoob Al Baqali ( The Chairman), and Mr. Mohsen Darwish ( The Managing Director). The Company is known in the market for offering a wide variety of automatic systems for gates and roll up shutters used for industrial and residential usage.
